- **Innovation** — Building deeply integrated AI solutions, not surface-level add-ons.

Horizon Tech was founded on a simple frustration: the software market is saturated with generic, subscription-based tools wrapped in a thin layer of "customization." Most established companies are reluctant to build AI deeply into a client's codebase — the risk feels too high for them to take on.

The founding team — some still studying, some with prior industry experience — came together around a shared belief that there had to be a better way. Being small means Horizon Tech can pursue the kind of deep, ground-up AI integration that larger, more risk-averse companies won't attempt.

## 2. Services

### Custom Software Development *(Core)*
Full bespoke software built from scratch around a client's specific workflow. Covers both customer-facing applications and internal operational systems — CRM-style lead management, attendance, performance tracking, payments, hierarchy management.

### AI-Integrated Solutions *(Core Differentiator)*
AI is built **directly into the codebase** with access to the client's actual data and workflows — enabling deeper automation, predictive analytics, and performance insights. Not bolted on top. Not a chatbot wrapper.

### MVP Development *(Active)*
A focused, core-feature version of a product built quickly and affordably to validate market fit.

### UI/UX Design *(Bundled)*
Included as part of custom development engagements. Not offered as a standalone service.

### Not Currently Offered
- **Maintenance & Security** — Available via trusted partner vendors. Horizon Tech connects clients directly.
- **DevOps / Cloud Migration** — Currently outsourced. Planned in-house once team expands.
- **API Development & Staff Augmentation** — Under consideration for future growth.

## 12. FAQs

**What is custom software development?**
Building software entirely from scratch around your specific needs and workflow, rather than adapting an existing generic product.

**How is your AI integration different from other companies?**
Most companies add AI as a layer on top of existing software (e.g. a simple chatbot). Horizon Tech builds AI directly into the core codebase with access to your actual data and workflows, enabling deeper automation and insight.

**How long does a typical project take?**
Most custom projects take three to six months, depending on complexity and scope.

**Do you sign NDAs?**
Yes. Horizon Tech signs NDAs to protect client confidentiality.

**What if we need to change project requirements mid-way?**
Scope changes are accommodated. Timeline and cost are adjusted accordingly and agreed with the client before proceeding.

**Who owns the code after the project is delivered?**
The client owns all source code on delivery, along with full documentation, so they can maintain or extend it independently.

**Do you work with international clients?**
Yes. Horizon Tech works with clients globally and adapts communication and scheduling accordingly.

**Can you integrate with our existing systems?**
It depends on project scope. Compatibility is assessed during the discovery phase and confirmed as part of the proposal.

**Do you offer maintenance and security after launch?**
Horizon Tech partners with trusted vendors for ongoing maintenance and security and connects clients with them directly.
